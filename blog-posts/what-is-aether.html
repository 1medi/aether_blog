What is Aether?

By Madi Tabon | Last Edited: October 15, 2024, 5:35 PM

Our project team is tackling the issues that seniors are facing regarding important documents. By using A.I, older individuals can have an easier time reading and comprehending important documents that they would normally have issues with.

The current state of the project is in early development with initial planning and role orientation in progress.

Saving Caretakers' Time, So They Can Focus On What Matters Most.
