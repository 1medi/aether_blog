Aether's Key Values

By Parnell Tse | Last Edited: October 14, 2024, 5:35 PM

Our Key Values

Here at Aether, we are developing our app with a focus on our key values. We have three key values that we are focusing on:

  • Simplify
  • Summarize
  • Streamline

Simplify

To make a process and/or task easier for the user. Reducing complexity and delivering straightforward solutions.

To simplify our app, we are going to:

  • Create simple and easy-to-understand UX and UI.
  • Incorporate time savers to reduce the amount of time spent on completing tasks, making it more efficient.
  • Ensure the app is accessible to users by removing barriers that may cause issues.

Summarize

To make complex information easier to understand and turn it into manageable parts. This will give users more clarity on tasks.

To implement this key value, we are going to:

  • Condense information by reducing lengthy text and making it more manageable.
  • Highlight and emphasize key points that are important so users can find what they are looking for right away.

Streamline

To create an experience that is smooth from beginning to end, making the process fast and efficient for users.

To streamline our app and document-filling process, we are going to:

  • Optimize processes by removing unnecessary steps.
  • Simplify error prevention and handling processes.
  • Consolidate all information into one easy-to-access place for the user.

Saving Caretakers' Time, So They Can Focus On What Matters Most.
